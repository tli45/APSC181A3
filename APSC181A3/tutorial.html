Step-by-Step Ollie Tutorial

Learn the Ollie in simple, beginner-friendly steps.

Step 1: Stance

Place your back foot on the tail of the board and your front foot just behind the front bolts. Stay balanced with knees bent.

Step 2: Pop

Push down hard with your back foot to pop the tail against the ground. This is the first motion that lifts the board.

Step 3: Slide

As the board rises, drag your front foot up toward the nose to level out the board. This motion controls stability.

Step 4: Tuck

As you jump, tuck your knees up towards your chest, and lifting back foot up again. This trick helps you jumper higher!

Step 5: Land

Use both feet to flatten the board mid-air and prepare to land with knees slightly bent, absorbing impact over the bolts.

Step 6: Welldone

Congratulations! You've completed a perfect skateboard ollie by your own! Time to show it off to your friends!
